55 Scimatics II

15th-17th November, 2019

About Us

Lahore Grammar School 55 Main welcomes you to the second and revamped edition of 55 Scimatics, held this November. 55 Main’s Scimath Society has evinced itself as a force to be reckoned with, both locally and internationally. From winning numerable Best Delegation awards at inter-school competitions to partaking in the National Science Talent Contest, we have been at the forefront of most science competitions. Time and time again, we aim for the stars by consistently qualifying for the semi-finals at Asian Regional Space Settlement Design Competition and winning the NASA Ames in 2019.

Our experience, coupled with our ideals for the future, has enabled us to lay down a blueprint for what we believe is an event like no other. With that in mind, you should know that the periphery of your ability will be put to the test; whether it stretches or shatters depends on how much you are willing to learn.

Why 55 Scimatics II

Introducing the heart of Scimatics: a total of thirteen invigorating sub-events, that will be carried out over a span of three adrenaline stimulating days. Each category has been prepared through rigorous planning and inspection to pose challenges that will have a delegate’s gears turning. These categories give students a level playing field in terms of opportunities-something that is often overlooked. With that in mind, we aim to create an environment where students are tested on the basis of their knowledge, practical skills, and communication when it comes to joint effort.

We believe the effort that went into the fashioning of this event will prove to be meritorious soon and hope that we sound convincing enough for you to ensure your attendance. So join us from the 15th to 17th of November with the assurance that this is an experience you don't want to miss out on.
